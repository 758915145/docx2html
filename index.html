docX to (very basic) HTML

Updated: 12 January 2018

Get an HTML version of a Word document. The docx file is processed in your browser (nothing is sent to a server). The end result will be very bare-bones HTML and it won't have styling. The result won't be perfect. It doesn't support images at the moment and links are not correctly created. Those things will be worked on.
